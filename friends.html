During my time at MRSM Mersing, I was fortunate to meet two of my best friends who have played an important role in my life. We bonded over shared experiences, laughter, and academic challenges, and those friendships have remained strong even after we went our separate ways for further studies. Their presence has been a constant source of support and encouragement throughout the years.

Nor Aina Shofia Bt Norazizoliraman

The first friend I want to talk about is Nor Aina Shofia binti Norazizoliraman. From the moment we met, I was drawn to her warm and caring nature. I remember seeing the first day I stepped into MRSM Mersing. She is incredibly dedicated and hardworking, qualities that have taken her far in life. After finishing at MRSM, Aina pursued her degree in education (Mathematics) with Honors at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI). Until now, she has never failed to amaze us with her educational achievements as she is known as the most genius among us. Seeing her dedication to her studies and her passion for education has always inspired me. Despite her busy schedule, we still keep in touch, still try to meet each other every semester break, and I am proud to see her chasing her dreams.

Nur Anis Sofia Bt Adisiman

My other best friend is Nur Anis Sofia bt Adisiman, who is just as remarkable. Anis has a vibrant and energetic personality that always brings a positive atmosphere wherever she goes. She went on to study for a diploma in Applied Science at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM). Anis’s passion science and her determination to succeed have always impressed me, and I know she’s destined for great things. She is the strongest soldier in our groups even if she looks very polite and gentle among us three. Even though we still didn’t get the chance to meet each other yet, there are still numerous videocalls we got to do every day. When we are reminiscing about our MRSM days or talking about our current lives, she never fails to lift my spirits.

Both Aina and Anis are not just friends but sisters I’ve gained along my journey. Even though we are all pursuing different paths, the bond we built during our time at MRSM Mersing remains strong. I am grateful to have these two amazing individuals in my life, and I know that, no matter where life takes us, our friendship will continue to grow.
